What is this workshop about?

In this course, you will build a Open Source Bionic Arm with 10 degrees of freedom which can be controlled by a smart phone using an Android App or EMG Signals. The Bionic Arm that you build will be able to do the action of Pick, Place and hand gesture. By building this project, you will practically learn about Arduino programming, EMG Sensors, working of the servo motors - which acts as actuators and bluetooth communication.

Course Highlights

  • Design and Build a Robotic Arm
  • Learn about Servo Motors and Pulse Width Modulation
  • Concepts of prosthetics and joints
  • Hands-on experience on Arduino and its programming
  • Electromyography Signals and processing

Course Structure

  • Lecture Session - 4 hrs
  • Design Session - 1 hr
  • Fabrication Session - 3 hrs
  • Programming Session - 4 hrs
  • Testing & Competition Session - 3.5 hrs
  • Certificate Distribution - 0.5 hr

Topics Covered

  • Bionic Arm: Concepts and Applications of prosthetics
  • HC-05 bluetooth module - concept and their working
  • Servo Motors: Working and Control of servo drivers
  • Arduino Architecture & its programming
  • Fabrication and 3D printing of Bionic Arm

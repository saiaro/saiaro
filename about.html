About Us

We're The Best Online Course Platform

Sai Automation Industry and Robotics Organization (SAIARO) is work in field of Automation and Robotics Education.

The aim of SAIARO is to teach Indian students about robotics and future technology and to take them step ahead in the field of engineering and automation.

Children are taught to learn programing and robotics with the help of varieties of open source development boards like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, etc. We also train students for industrials project by giving them a opportunity to work with us.

We provide door step trainer and conduct on demand workshop at campus to teach robotics to every single child about robotics.

Competitions like Robo-War, Robo-Soccer,Model Rocket, App Developement are also held time to time with exciting winning prizes.

Hands on project agenda of SAIARO motivate students to learn more about robotics and science.

We also provide fundings and sponsorships for projects and aim to bring the best out of everything.
